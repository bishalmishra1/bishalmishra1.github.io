About Us:

Welcome to Guide Globe Education Consultancy, an esteemed pillar in the realm of educational guidance, situated in Putalisadak, Kathmandu. For over a decade, we have been the trusted bridge between ambitious students and prestigious colleges and universities worldwide. Our legacy is backed by an impressive visa success rate, a testament to our meticulous and personalized approach. With a team of the finest counselors, each specializing in different countries, we ensure that every student's dream destination is matched with unparalleled expertise. Choose Guide Globe Education Consultancy, where your global academic aspirations are transformed into successful realities.

Our Vision
